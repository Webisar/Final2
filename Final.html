About Muyembe Health Centre IV

Muyembe Health Centre IV is a government health facility located in Bulambuli District, Eastern Uganda. We provide quality healthcare services to the community with a focus on maternal and child health, infectious diseases, and general medical care.

Established in 2005, we have grown to become one of the leading health centres in the region, serving over 50,000 patients annually.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable and quality healthcare services to all people in Bulambuli and neighboring communities.

Our Vision

A healthy community with improved quality of life through preventive, promotive and curative healthcare services.
